  • Cosmetology

    1600 hour course

    Cosmetology covers all technical and practical subjects of Cosmetology as prescribed by the criteria set forth by the California State Board of Barbering and Cosmetology.

    Course Overview

    • class Hours

      Full Time 10 Month Course

      Tuesday–Friday / 9:30 AM−6:00 PM
      Saturdays / 9:00 AM−5:30 PM

    • class Hours

      Part Time 17–18 Month Course

      Tuesday−Friday / 9:30 AM−2:30 PM
      Alternating Saturday(s) / 9:00 AM−2:00 PM

    Our curriculum includes areas such as:

    • Hair Cutting
      Hair Design
      Hair Color
      Permanent Waving
      Long Hair Design
    • Chemical Straightening
      Multi-cultural
      Hair Design
      Manicures & Pedicures
    • Artificial Nails
      Facials
      Waxing
      Make-up application
      Business Development
      People Skills
    • State Board Preparation
      Advanced Hair Color
      Classes with Framesi Color

    At completion, students will receive a Diploma and will be certified to take the California State Board Examination for Cosmetology. Upon successful completion of the exam, you will receive a license to practice Cosmetology.

    Tuition $13,600
    Books/Kit $3,672
    Registration fee $100
    Total price $17,372
