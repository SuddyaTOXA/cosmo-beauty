About Cosmo

Cosmo Beauty Academy is a comprehensive, high tech institute that offers state-of-the-art equipment and experienced staff for future professionals in the field of cosmetology, esthetics, and nail technology by providing various programs.

Why Choose Us?

At our institute, we strive to provide you with the following:

  • Experienced staff dedicated to our mission and delivery of top-quality education tailored to your individual needs.
  • Hands on training provides you with the opportunity to practice on all textured hair, nail, and skin types.
  • Field experience in salons for exposure to the actual profession and opportunistic future employers.
  • Organized and structured programs with available learning assistance.
  • Affordable tuition.
  • Continuous progress monitoring and constructive feedback structured to allow a passing grade on the California State Board of Barber and Cosmetology exam.

OUR Mission

  • To produce students who will have the required talent, skills, and knowledge for both the technical and practical portions of the course so that upon graduation they will be prepared to pass the written and practical examinations administered by the State Board of Cosmetology.
  • To develop desirable work habits, attitude, and pride in workmanship.
  • To provide quality progressive education preparing students for a career in the field of Cosmetology, Esthetics, and Manicuring.
  • To help students obtain employment in an entry level position upon graduation and licensing.
