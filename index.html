Welcome

We are a vocational beauty school located in Sacramento that instructs in Cosmetology, Esthetics, and Manicuring. Cosmo Beauty Academy first opened its doors in January 2007 and within the span of 5 years, we have established accreditation from the National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts & Sciences, the Bureau for Private Post-secondary Education, California State Board of Barbering & Cosmetology, and Title IV Funding from the Department of Education. The field of cosmetology is ever-expanding. Whether as a professional Make-up Artist, Master Hair Stylist or Skin Care Specialist, you will find yourself engaged in a career that is dynamic, rewarding, and in-demand.
